KeysCleaner - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2024

At KeysCleaner ("we", "us", or "our"), we respect and prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our macOS application, KeysCleaner, handles information related to its usage and features. By using KeysCleaner, you agree to the terms and practices described here.

1. Information We Access

KeysCleaner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or identifying information. The application operates locally on your device and does not require access to your personal data, files, or network activity.

2. How We Use Your Device’s Input

KeysCleaner temporarily disables keyboard input for a selected duration to allow you to clean your keyboard without triggering unwanted keystrokes. This functionality is performed locally on your machine and does not involve sending any data to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Collection, Sharing, and Disclosure

KeysCleaner does not collect or share any information about your usage, input events, or other personal details. Since no data is gathered, there is nothing to disclose to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Because KeysCleaner does not store any user data, you do not need to worry about data security within the application. All processes occur within the confines of your local macOS environment, benefiting from the device’s inherent security measures.

5. Third-Party Services

KeysCleaner does not integrate with, rely on, or communicate with any third-party services. The app runs entirely on your device and no external calls are made.

6. Children’s Privacy

KeysCleaner is not targeted at children and does not collect any data, including from children under the age of 13. Without data collection, the app poses no risk to child privacy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
