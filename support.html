Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. How do I pause my keyboard?
    In KeysCleaner’s main window, select your preferred duration and click "Start". Your keyboard will be blocked for the chosen time. To stop early, simply click the "Stop" button.
  • 2. Can I stop the blocking before the timer ends?
    Yes. Click the "Stop" button at any time and your keyboard will be re-enabled immediately.
  • 3. Do I need special permissions?
    KeysCleaner requires Accessibility permissions to temporarily block your keyboard input. The app will prompt you to grant these permissions in System Settings if you haven’t done so already.
  • 4. Is any data collected or sent to servers?
    No. KeysCleaner does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. Everything happens locally on your device.
  • 5. Will KeysCleaner work with external keyboards?
    KeysCleaner is designed to block the keyboard events at a system level. Most external keyboards connected to your Mac will also be blocked during the set duration.
  • 6. How do I know when the timer is finished?
    When the timer counts down to zero, keyboard input is automatically restored, and the app will display "Keyboard is active" to indicate that normal typing can resume.
  • 7. Can I customize the available durations?
    Currently, KeysCleaner offers predefined intervals (e.g., 15s, 30s, 1m). Future updates may include additional customization options.
